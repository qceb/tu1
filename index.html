[Integral Fast Terminal Sliding Mode Control for Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor Speed Regulation System Based on Fixed-Time Disturbance Observer] - Experimental video

author：[Junwen Tu]

institution：[Nanjing Agricultural University]

Overview of the experiment

The experimental video comprises three parts: the first part provides an overview of the experimental environment and system configuration, demonstrating the permanent magnet synchronous motor (PMSM) test platform employed in this study, which primarily consists of a dynamic torque sensor, hardware components (featuring a TI DSP C2000 series-based control circuit and a TI DRV8305 chip-based power circuit), MATLAB-based host computer, and a PMSM, forming a complete motor drive system designed to achieve efficient and high-dynamic control of the three-phase PMSM; the second part presents a comparative experimental analysis of no-load startup speed performance under three different control strategies; the third part conducts a comparative experimental investigation of disturbance rejection performance under three distinct control methods.
